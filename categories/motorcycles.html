Ducati Heritage

Founded in 1926 in Bologna, Italy, Ducati began as a company producing radio components before turning to motorcycle production after World War II. Their first motorcycle, the Cucciolo, was a small engine attached to a bicycle.

Throughout the decades, Ducati established itself as a premium motorcycle manufacturer known for innovative engineering, distinctive design, and racing success. The brand has become synonymous with Italian craftsmanship and passion for performance.

Today, Ducati continues to push boundaries in motorcycle technology while maintaining their commitment to style, performance, and the unmistakable sound of their engines.
