Ducati Panigale V4

The ultimate expression of performance and Italian engineering - a MotoGP-derived masterpiece for the road

Engineering Excellence

The Panigale V4 represents the pinnacle of Ducati's racing technology transferred to a road-legal superbike. With its distinctive design, cutting-edge technology, and race-bred performance, it embodies the perfect balance of power and elegance.

Remarkable Features

Every aspect of the Panigale V4 has been meticulously engineered to deliver uncompromising performance on both road and track.

Desmosedici Stradale V4

The 1,103cc 90° V4 engine delivers 214 hp with a spine-tingling exhaust note, derived directly from Ducati's MotoGP technology.

Race-Inspired Aerodynamics

Features winglets that generate 30kg of downforce at 270 km/h, improving stability and front-end grip at high speeds.

Advanced Electronics Package

State-of-the-art electronics including cornering ABS, traction control, wheelie control, and six-axis IMU for optimal performance.

Technical Specifications

The numbers behind the performance that makes the Panigale V4 one of the most formidable motorcycles on the planet.

Engine Type Desmosedici Stradale 90° V4
Displacement 1,103 cc
Power Output 214 hp @ 13,000 rpm
Torque 91.5 lb-ft @ 9,500 rpm
Dry Weight 175 kg (386 lbs)
Top Speed 305+ km/h (190+ mph)
Frame Aluminum front frame
Electronics 6 riding modes, cornering ABS, DTC, DWC, EBC, DQS
